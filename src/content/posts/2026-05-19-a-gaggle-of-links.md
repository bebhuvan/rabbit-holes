>
> Germany, Czechia, Slovakia, and Hungary specialized in the production of internal combustion engine cars, and thus were more exposed to China’s remarkable emergence as the [biggest auto exporter the world has ever known](https://www.cfr.org/articles/will-china-take-over-global-auto-industry).
