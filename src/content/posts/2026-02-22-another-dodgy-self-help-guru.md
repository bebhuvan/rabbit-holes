What fascinates me is why such people who loosely preach some form of "self-help" become popular despite the questionable nature of their advice, their disingenuousness, and the outright grift. What fascinates me is the continued popularity of self-help despite the painfully bland advice. The fact that books that tell you to fold your blanket after you wake up and wash your ass after taking a shit continue to be bestsellers is funny and tragic at the same time.


I get that these books, talks, and podcasts make it seem like they have answers and there's money to be made in exploiting people's hopes, fears, dreams, insecurities, and anxieties.
